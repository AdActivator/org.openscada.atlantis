The OpenSCADA RCP Client is split up in several perspectives. Each perspective focuses on one particular topic of the OpenSCADA system.

The following perspecives are available:

Client Perspective
The client perspective shows only minimal functionality. It acts as an entry point to all other functionality.
DA Perspective
The DA (DataAccess) perspective covers the OpenSCADA DA Interface. This is what was previously the the OpenSCADA Test Client.
AE Perspective
The AE (Alarms & Events) perspective covers the OpenSCADA AE Interface. It is the equivalent to the DA perspective for Alarms & Events.
